Blog post one

Post by SABIR KHAN in January

Every sentence we use has the purpose of enabling second language learners to move beyond vocabulary and grammar to the functional aspect of communication. Being able to communicate with each other form bonds, teamwork, and it’s what separates humans from other animal species. Communication drives our lives and better ourselves. The topics and the exercises of this text aim at enhancement of the learners’ communication skill. Let’s begin with ‘Self-introduction’ as “You only get one chance to create a first and best impression”. The arrival of a new academic year brings a lot of energy and enthusiasm to students, teachers and parents (although in a different way) and it is high time to practise.
